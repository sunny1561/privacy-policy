Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 25, 2025

This application uses Google OAuth to authenticate users and access Gmail APIs for reading and sending emails on behalf of the user.

Information We Collect

Upon Google login, we collect the following:

  • User's email address and name
  • Access tokens to perform Gmail operations (read/send email)

How We Use the Information

We use the access tokens solely to:

  • Read recent incoming emails
  • Send emails on behalf of the authenticated user
This is required for the core functionality of our AI-powered Email CRM application.

Data Retention

We do not store any user emails or Gmail content on our servers. Access tokens are stored securely and used temporarily during the session.

Sharing of Information

We do not share any user data with third parties.
